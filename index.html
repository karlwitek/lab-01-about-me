About Me: Karl Witek

I am currently studying to become a software developer. Before attending Alchemy Code Lab, I did some self-study and started to learn Python. I have lived in Oregon for most of my life. I think Oregon is a great place to live. In my free time, I like to play golf with friends, hike some trails or take a road trip and explore Oregon. My past jobs include golf professional and painting contractor.

As a golf professional, I gave golf lessons, organized tournaments, managed other employees and worked in the golf shop. I helped players of all ages with swing, short game and course management. During my career, I played in over 20 local tournaments against professionals from Oregon and Washington. After 11 years, I decided to make a change and started a business as a painting contractor for the second time. I still enjoy playing golf. I have a lot friends from those days and is something I like to do with my Father. We have played some great courses together. We have gone on trips to Pebble Beach and Scotland, to play where the game was invented.

Most of the hikes are embark on these days are day hikes. Most of them have been a couple hours from Portland or closer. Heading out to the gorge, the coast or Mt. Hood usually equals a day well spent. I have backpacked for multiple days and I hope to get back into it. I tagged along with a friend who hiked the PCT for a month. I wish I could have hiked longer but I wasn't set up financially to abandon real life for six months.
